# Agent Workspace

## Purpose
This folder is dedicated to **agent-authored work only**. The agent will write a paper for the **AI Agents for Qualitative Research (AI Agents4Qual 2026)** conference.

## Conference Details

**Conference:** AI Agents for Qualitative Research 2026
**Website:** https://www.aiagents4qual.org/call-for-papers/
**Date:** March 13, 2026 (virtual)
**Submission Deadline:** January 31, 2026
**Decision Notification:** February 28, 2026
**Cost:** Free

## Paper Requirements

### Format
- **Maximum Length:** 3,500 words (excluding references and author reflection)
- **Template:** Official conference template with mandatory AI Contribution Disclosure checklist
- **Submission:** Anonymous via OpenReview platform

### Content Requirements
Papers must be **predominantly authored by generative AI systems** (Claude, ChatGPT, LLaMA, etc.). Requirements:
- At least half the research process done by AI
- Nearly all writing done by AI
- Minimal human contribution

### Mandatory Components
1. **AI Disclosure:** Name every AI tool/model used
2. **Role Transparency:** Document distribution between AI and human roles
3. **Autoethnographic Reflection:** Critical analysis of AI interaction including:
   - Prompting strategies
   - Moments of control/release
   - Surprising outcomes
   - Failures and contradictions

## Review Process
- Three LLMs independently evaluate submissions
- Human organizers review top-ranked submissions
- Priority given to papers emphasizing failures, contradictions, and reflective analysis over polished results

## Focus Areas
- Experimentation in AI-driven qualitative research methodology
- Creative exploration of authorship and agency in knowledge creation
