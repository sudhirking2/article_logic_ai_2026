Before entering the laboratory, all researchers must wear protective equipment and sign the safety logbook.
After completing an experiment, the workspace must be cleaned and all chemicals must be properly stored.
While the fume hood is running, the ventilation system cannot be turned off.
Senior researchers typically supervise undergraduate students during their first month, though this is not always enforced.
Lab equipment should be inspected weekly, but monthly inspections are generally acceptable for low-risk items.
If a chemical spill occurs, the area must be evacuated immediately and the safety officer must be notified within 5 minutes.
Researchers are encouraged to attend monthly safety meetings, although attendance is not mandatory for experienced personnel.
The lab director believes that all experiments involving hazardous materials should be conducted with a partner present, but researchers working after hours sometimes work alone.
